# Welcome to SkyBlock Enhanced!

Thank you for installing **SkyBlock Enhanced**!

This pack is the result of hundreds of hours over the past year: everything from building custom mods, testing every mod against the others so nothing breaks, dialing in default configs so you can jump in and go, and answering thousands of questions on the Discord along the way.

It's been a labor of love, and I hope it gives you an Enhanced SkyBlock experience.

If you'd like to help keep it going, the buttons on the right have a few ways to show your support (100% optional). Either way, thanks for being here and have fun!
