# Performance Profiles

SkyBlock Enhanced ships optimised out of the box, but there is always some tuning that can be done — things like render distance and quality presets make a real difference. Pick the profile that best matches your hardware. You can always change it later with `/packcore performance <profile>`.

On the images below you can see the FPS I get on the different profiles. I'm running a Ryzen 7 5700X3D and an RX 9070 XT, so results may differ significantly for you.

---

## Max FPS

Best for older laptops or low-end hardware. Cuts visual effects to squeeze out the most frames possible.

![Max FPS Preview](packcore:textures/gui/performance/max_fps.png 2560x1440)

**Settings applied:**
- Graphics: Fast
- View Distance: 10 chunks
- Simulation Distance: 10 chunks
- Entity Shadows: Off
- Particles: Minimal
- Clouds: Fast
- Biome Blending: Reduced

---

## Balanced

Recommended for most players. A solid middle ground between visuals and performance.

![Balanced Preview](packcore:textures/gui/performance/balanced.png 2560x1440)

**Settings applied:**
- Graphics: Fancy
- View Distance: 16 chunks
- Simulation Distance: 12 chunks
- Entity Shadows: On
- Particles: All
- Clouds: Fancy

---

## Quality

For mid-range to higher-end systems that want the best visuals without shaders.

![Quality Preview](packcore:textures/gui/performance/quality.png 2560x1440)

**Settings applied:**
- Graphics: Fabulous
- View Distance: 20 chunks
- Simulation Distance: 12 chunks
- Entity Distance Scaling: 125%
- Enhanced lighting and shadows

---

## Balanced + Performance Shaders

Adds lightweight shaders on top of the Balanced preset. Visual polish without a heavy FPS cost.

![Balanced Shaders Preview](packcore:textures/gui/performance/balanced_shaders.png 2560x1440)

**Shader pack:** MakeUp - Ultra Fast

> **Note:** Some mod features like waypoints or in-world overlays may not display correctly with shaders enabled. If that happens, toggle shaders off with **K**.

---

## Quality + Quality Shaders

The full visual experience. Requires a capable GPU.

![Quality Shaders Preview](packcore:textures/gui/performance/quality_shaders.png 2560x1440)

**Shader pack:** Complementary Unbound

> **Note:** Some mod features like waypoints or in-world overlays may not display correctly with shaders enabled. If that happens, toggle shaders off with **K**.
