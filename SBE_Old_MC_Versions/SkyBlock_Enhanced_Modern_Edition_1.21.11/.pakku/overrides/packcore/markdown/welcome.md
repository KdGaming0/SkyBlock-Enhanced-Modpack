# Welcome!

You've just installed **SkyBlock Enhanced**!

Let's walk through a quick setup to make your Hypixel SkyBlock experience smooth and personal.

> To reopen this menu later do `/packcore wizard` in chat

---

## What to expect

**SkyBlock Enhanced** comes packed with quality-of-life mods to improve every part of the game. With so many mods, things can overlap or clash — the fine-tuning is already done so everything works well together.

The pack is **pre-configured for your resolution.** Each resolution needs unique configs since overlays shift with screen size. Ready-made configs are included for **1080p, 1440p, and 4K.** The modpack checks your resolution on first launch and applies the closest one automatically. A small box on the right shows which config loaded and whether it worked.

Got the wrong one? Select one of the others from the list on the right. You can also set the GUI scale to 2x instead of the default 3x for 1080p, or import community ones from [our Discord](https://discord.gg/pdwxyjTta7).

---

## What you'll set up now

1. Choose a **main menu design** — Vanilla, Modern, or Minimal
2. Choose a **video preset** — Max FPS, Balanced, Quality, or Shaders
3. Pick a **tab list design** — SkyHanni (compact) or SkyBlocker (fancy)
4. Choose an **item background** style — None, Circle, or Square
5. Choose a **storage display** style — Overlay or Vanilla
6. Select any **resource packs** you want
7. **Review and apply** everything in one click
