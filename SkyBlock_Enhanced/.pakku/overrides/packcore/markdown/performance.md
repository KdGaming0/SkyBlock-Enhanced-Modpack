# Performance Profiles

SkyBlock Enhanced runs optimised out of the box — but a little tuning goes a long way. Things like render distance and quality presets make a real difference, so pick the profile that matches your hardware and you're set.

Changed your mind later? Swap any time with `/packcore performance <profile>`.

> The FPS shown below is what I get on a Ryzen 7 5700X3D and an RX 9070 XT — your numbers will vary depending on your setup. (The images are outdated, and you can as of verison 5.0.0 expect even better performance across the board thanks to better optimisations.)

---

## Max FPS

Built for older laptops and low-end hardware. Trims the visuals to squeeze out every last frame.

![Max FPS Preview](packcore:textures/gui/performance/max_fps.png 2560x1440)

**Settings applied:**
- Graphics: Fast
- View Distance: 10 chunks
- Simulation Distance: 10 chunks
- Entity Shadows: Off
- Particles: Minimal
- Clouds: Fast
- Biome Blending: Reduced

---

## Balanced

The sweet spot, and what most players should pick. Great visuals, smooth frames.

![Balanced Preview](packcore:textures/gui/performance/balanced.png 2560x1440)

**Settings applied:**
- Graphics: Fancy
- View Distance: 16 chunks
- Simulation Distance: 12 chunks
- Entity Shadows: On
- Particles: All
- Clouds: Fancy

---

## Quality

For mid-range and higher-end systems that want the best look — no shaders required.

![Quality Preview](packcore:textures/gui/performance/quality.png 2560x1440)

**Settings applied:**
- Graphics: Fabulous
- View Distance: 20 chunks
- Simulation Distance: 12 chunks
- Entity Distance Scaling: 125%
- Enhanced lighting and shadows

---

## Balanced + Performance Shaders

The Balanced preset with lightweight shaders on top — extra polish, barely any FPS cost.

![Balanced Shaders Preview](packcore:textures/gui/performance/balanced_shaders.png 2560x1440)

**Shader pack:** MakeUp – Ultra Fast

> **Heads up:** waypoints and other in-world overlays may not render correctly with shaders on. If that happens, toggle shaders off with **K**.
